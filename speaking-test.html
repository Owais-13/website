IELTS Speaking Mock Test

Complete all three parts of the speaking test. Record your responses or type them below for AI analysis.

Part 1: Introduction and Interview (4-5 minutes)

Answer questions about familiar topics

Topic: Your Studies/Work
  • What do you do? Do you work or are you a student?
  • What subject are you studying? / What is your job?
  • Why did you choose this subject/job?
  • What do you like most about it?
00:00

Part 2: Individual Long Turn (3-4 minutes)

Speak about a topic for 1-2 minutes after 1 minute preparation

Topic Card

Describe a book that you enjoyed reading

You should say:

  • What the book was about
  • When you read it
  • Why you decided to read it
  • And explain why you enjoyed it
00:00

Part 3: Two-way Discussion (4-5 minutes)

Discuss abstract ideas related to the Part 2 topic

Discussion Questions
  • What kinds of books are most popular in your country?
  • Do you think reading books is more important now than in the past?
  • How do you think technology has changed the way people read?
  • What are the advantages of e-books over traditional books?
  • Do you think books will disappear in the future?
00:00
