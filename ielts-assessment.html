Reading Assessment

Read the following passage and answer the questions.

Passage: The Impact of Climate Change on Global Agriculture

Climate change represents one of the most significant challenges facing global agriculture in the 21st century. Rising temperatures, changing precipitation patterns, and increased frequency of extreme weather events are already affecting crop yields and agricultural productivity worldwide.

Research indicates that global crop yields could decline by 5-15% by 2050 due to climate change, with developing countries being particularly vulnerable. However, the impact varies significantly by region and crop type. Some areas may actually benefit from longer growing seasons, while others face increased drought and flooding risks.

Adaptation strategies are crucial for maintaining food security. These include developing drought-resistant crop varieties, improving irrigation systems, and implementing sustainable farming practices. Governments and international organizations are increasingly recognizing the need for coordinated action to address these challenges.

Question 1: What is the main focus of the passage?
Question 2: According to the passage, what percentage decline in crop yields is predicted by 2050?
Question 3: Which of the following is mentioned as an adaptation strategy?
